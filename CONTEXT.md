# Dailytech

A modern platform providing intuitive, beautiful, and deeply personal AI solutions and tools for people's day-to-day lives.

## Language

**Dailytech**:
The parent platform and brand focused on designing personal AI companions and day-to-day lifestyle utilities.
_Avoid_: DailyTech Corp, daily-tech

**Mirre**:
A mobile-first fashion styling and Virtual Try-On (VTO) commerce application.
_Avoid_: Mirre Mobile, VTO App

**Loomin**:
An AI-driven digital wardrobe assistant that curates outfits based on weather, schedule, and personal energy levels.
_Avoid_: Loomin Stylist, Wardrobe Planner

**Cura**:
An AI spatial and interior designer that suggests decor changes and visualizes room layouts.
_Avoid_: Cura Design, Interior AI

**Kairos**:
A cognitive-aware daily scheduler that aligns personal tasks with natural focus peaks and circadian rhythms.
_Avoid_: Kairos Calendar, Daily Scheduler

**Virtual Try-On (VTO)**:
The neural rendering process that visualizes garments on a user's digital likeness.
_Avoid_: Digital Dressing, Fit Check

## Example Dialogue

**Developer**: "Are we adding the digital wardrobe feature to the Mirre mobile app?"
**Domain Expert**: "No, that belongs to Loomin. Loomin handles the ongoing daily scheduling and wardrobe organization, whereas Mirre focus exclusively on Virtual Try-On (VTO) from brand catalogs."
